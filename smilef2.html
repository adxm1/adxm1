Oldtimer1's Smile — F2 Open Pollination

Welcome! I started 14 Smile F1 seeds, 13 germinated. Seeds were 15 years old. I will post updates here.

Oldtimer was a UK grower seed collector and breeder with a love of sativas and a very high level of intelligence. He worked for decades, from when only a very few people gkobally knew it was even possible to grow good cannabis indoorsa. in England and produced several strains for release. Smile is his most indica plant but retains, in his words, the 'sativa spirit'. I bought Smile Oldtimes C3 and Smellberry around 2009-11. These are the last f1 Smile seeds I have. Hopefully I'll be able to retain all the plants in open pollination, to preserve as much genetic diversity as possible from this small number. Already you can see extraordinary vigour and germination rate still in the seed. Space is limited so it will be interesting if nothing else!
