Oldtimer1's Smile — F2 Open Pollination

Welcome! I started 14 seeds, 13 germinated. Seeds were 15 years old. I will post updates here.

Oldtimer was a UK grower seed collector and breeder with a love of Sativas and a very high level of intelligence. He worked for decades in England and produced several strains for release. Smile is his most indica plant but retains, in his words, the 'sativa spirit'. I bought Smile Oldtimes C3 and Smellberry around 2010. These are the last f1 Smile seedsI have. I will hopefully retain all the plants in open pollination, to preserve as much genetic diversity as possible from this small number of plants. Already I think you can dee the quality in the plants and the extraordinary vigour still in the seed. Space is limited so it will be interesting if nothing else!
